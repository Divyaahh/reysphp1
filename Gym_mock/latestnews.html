Hyrox: The new fitness trend sweeping the capital

HYROX is a global fitness competition that combines endurance and strength training in a unique, standardized format. It's designed to challenge athletes of all fitness levels, and participants compete in a 8-station course, each testing different aspects of physical fitness. The competition is structured to be accessible, offering both individual and team categories, and it has grown in popularity due to its straightforward format and global appeal.
