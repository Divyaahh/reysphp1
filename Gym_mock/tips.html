Here are some tips for you to stay fit and healthy

Eat a variety of foods. This will help you get all the nutrients your body needs

Eat plenty of fruits and vegetables. Fruits and vegetables are low in calories and high in nutrients

Whole grains are a good source of fibre, which is important for digestion.

Limit unhealthy fats. Unhealthy fats can raise your cholesterol levels.

Limit added sugar and salt. Too much sugar and salt can lead to health problems.

Drink plenty of water. Water is essential for good health.

Choose lean protein sources. Lean protein sources include fish, poultry, beans, and nuts.

Limit processed foods. Processed foods are often high in unhealthy fats, sugar, and salt.
